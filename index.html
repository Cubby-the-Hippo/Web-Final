We are Fritz Reuter Stonemasonry

Our family-owned master business, which has been in existence since 1884, offers you competent and individual advice when purchasing a new gravestone, new inscriptions for your deceased loved ones and any repair or renovation work. We handle the approval procedures for all cemeteries in Munich and the surrounding area. Our exhibition area next door (Ungererstr. 116) shows you a variety of shapes, materials and processing options. One of our main focuses is the subsequent inscription by master craftsmen, be it chiselled and painted, laid out in lead, fine gold lettering or applied bronze and aluminum inscriptions on existing gravestones, which are always inspected beforehand in order to achieve the best possible result. Furthermore, as a guild company, we carry out professional repairs, cleaning and renovations on gravestones, wooden/wrought crosses and borders, as well as erecting and moving work. We would be happy to advise you on the search for suitable grave accessories (grave lanterns, holy water fonts, vases, etc.). We look forward to welcoming you to our office studio!
